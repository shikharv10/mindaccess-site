Frequently Asked Questions

What is generational or ancestral trauma?

Generational or ancestral trauma refers to patterns, emotions, and traumas passed through family bloodlines. Families genuinely pass down emotional patterns, coping mechanisms, fears, and unresolved traumas through generations. This concept is supported by emerging research in epigenetics, showing how family experiences and stress can affect gene expression across generations.

How do I know if I need ancestral healing?

You may benefit from ancestral healing if you experience recurring patterns that don't feel entirely yours, unexplained intense emotions toward certain people or situations, or family patterns of addiction, anger, fear, or anxiety that seem to run through multiple generations. These are often signs of inherited emotional and trauma bonds.

How many ancestral healing sessions will I need?

Most clients see significant results in 1 to 4 sessions. Our unhurried approach — with sessions averaging 3 hours each — means each session covers substantial ancestral healing work. The exact number depends on the depth and complexity of your family patterns, but our three-hour sessions are designed for comprehensive ancestral work.

With over 17 years of clinical experience and 2,500+ successfully treated patients, we ensure a safe and supportive environment for your ancestral healing journey.
