Breaking Out of Coma – Surrogate Therapy

A case study from the MindAccess practice

By: Dr. Shalini Verma Published: November 28, 2021 ← All Stories

One of the most tragic things that can happen to someone is when their loved one is in a coma, alive but lifeless, living but unconscious, that too for an indefinite period of time.

Mayank Tiwari was a 26-year-old man, full of dreams and enthusiasm. Ambitious, determined, independent, passionate, and creative.

He had an entrepreneurial zeal, he worked in the creative industry as a photographer. He was also a proud owner of a cafe called GOSSIP CORNER, which he had established in partnership with his friend.

Life took a U-turn and he was cheated in a business after which he suffered from stroke due to prolonged stress, on 3rd July 2021.

He had high blood pressure and his left side of the brain was affected. Meanwhile, doctors operated on him during which the blood vessels burst and internal bleeding started, doctors declared coma.

On 09.07.2021, our sessions started, we opted for Surrogate Healing Hypnotherapy wherein his mother, Mrs. Kiran Tiwari became our medium as Mr. Mayank could not be receptive to a direct session because of coma.

A Surrogate Healing Hypnosis session is for those who cannot undergo hypnosis for reasons such as age, disability, a medical condition, or someone with zero or negligible analytical ability.

One needs to be very close to the individual to help heal him/her through surrogate hypnosis; a parent, a partner, a sibling, an adult child, pretty much anyone close to them with a pure intention for their betterment. Surrogacy requires a deeply loving connection with the person who needs to be treated.

You may ask, how does such a method of therapy work? Everything in the material world is made of a vital force called “prana” in Sanskrit. Through the combination of ether and prana, we can manipulate the energies within an individual. A medium is required who should have a strong connection with the patient. That medium helps us in changing the energy level of the individual who needs to be healed.

Thoughts and emotions are energy as well (again a combination of ether and the vital force, i.e., prana) and these intentions magnify the vibration of healing energy for all involved.

After a few sessions, considerable improvement was noticed. Doctors were astonished and called it a miracle. The patient responded after 1 month 10 days approximately. Mayank has astonished everyone with his recovery, he is talking and responding, even walking with a stick. He is positive, hopeful, and energetic. Looking at his recovery rate, the day is not far wherein we won’t be able to spot a difference between the healthy Mayank and the Mayank post the stroke.
