Your Journey to Transformation

1

Reach Out

Message us on WhatsApp or call. Dr. Shalini will have a brief 10-minute conversation to understand what you're going through and guide you on the right next step.

Consultation Session

A dedicated one-hour session where Dr. Shalini listens deeply to your story — your background, your concerns, and what you're hoping to change. This is where we truly understand you.

Before any deep work begins, we explain exactly how hypnotherapy works, clear up any misconceptions, and prepare your mind for the main sessions. You'll go in feeling informed, comfortable, and ready.

The deep, transformative work. Using the right modality for your needs — hypnotherapy, past life regression, inner child healing, or others — Capt. Verma's custom-designed scripts guide each session. Sessions average 3 hours and continue until breakthrough, never cut short by a timer.
